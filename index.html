Aesthrellan Grip Status Meter of Alliance

(AMATRIX) | Revised as of June 28, 2026

The Aesthrellan Grip Status Meter on Alliance (AMATRIX) is the formal evaluation matrix used by the People's Republic of Aesthrella to measure the proximity, reliability, and ideological alignment of its allied micronations. Because Aesthrella is strictly committed to peaceful diplomacy, the scale measures alignment based on anti-war compliance, rendering the most aggressive, pro-war states as the farthest removed from the republic.
